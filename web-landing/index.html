FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is JustUs distributed via APK, not the Play Store?

+

Direct APK distribution allows us to ship hot patches directly to our Socket engine, maintain completely independent privacy metrics, and completely avoid corporate developer taxes and analytics SDK mandates.

Is it safe to install the JustUs APK?

+

Absolutely. Our codebase is open and contains zero malware, third-party trackers, or background packages. Android's warnings during APK installs are standard safety notifications for apps sideloaded outside official stores.

Does JustUs update automatically in real-time?

+

Yes. We utilize WebSocket streams, meaning updates, mood check-ins, and ping alerts register on your partner's dashboard in milliseconds—no manual reload required.

What happens if we disable Couple+ Mode?

+

JustUs defaults back to Standard Mode instantly. Couple+ premium fields (Bucket lists, Sync ratings, Prompts) are locked out, and the database status updates for both users immediately.
