+

Completely. JustUs contains no malware or hidden trackers. Android shows a standard safety prompt for all direct APK installs — this is normal behaviour for any app installed outside the Play Store.

Yes. Using raw WebSockets, any mood updates, quick pings, or daily love notes appear on your partner's device in milliseconds — no manual refresh needed, ever.
