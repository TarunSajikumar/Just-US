Tap the **"Download JustUs APK"** button on the website and save the `.apk` file directly to your Android device.

### **Step 2: Enable Installation from Unknown Sources**
*For security, Android requires manual approval for apps installed outside the Play Store:*
1. Locate the downloaded `.apk` file in your **Downloads** folder or tap the finished download notification.
2. Tap the file to install. If prompted by system settings, tap **Settings**.
3. Toggle on **"Allow from this source"** (or **"Unknown Sources"** depending on your Android version).

### **Step 3: Install & Open**
Go back to the installer screen and tap **Install**. Once finished, tap **Open** to launch **JustUs**.

### **Step 4: Pair with Your Partner**
